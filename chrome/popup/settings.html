Shared Video features

These options will only enable/disable the settings for both Prime Video and Netflix. If you want to only disable it specifically for one you have to go to its Individual settings.

Skip Intro & Recap:

Skipping the intros and recaps of any series. Except Netflix since there Recaps cannot be watched again.

Skip Credits:

Skipping to the next episode of every series as fast as possible

Skip Ads:

Skipping freevee ads for amazon and all of the ads in the basic Netflix tier

Add Speed Slider:

Adding a speed adjustment slider to every video

Play on Fullscreen:

If a video is put on Fullscreen, the video will play automatically
